Backstreet Boys About Top Hits Band's History

1993

The Backstreet Boys were formed in Orlando, Florida by
Lou Pearlan. The original members were AJ Mclean, Howie
Dorough, Nick Carterm Kevin Richardson, and Brian Littrell.
The boys performed their first big show at Sea World in Orlando, Florida
to a crowd of 3,000.

1996

The group became famous, with their international debut album, Backstreet Boys.
They won the MTV Select Award at the MTV European Music Awards for "Get Down "You're The One For Me.”

1997

They released their second album, Backstreets Back.
They also won the MTV Select Award at the MTV European Music Awards for "As Long As You Love Me."

1998

They won the following awards: Group Album of the Year at the Billboard Awards,
Peoples Choice Favorite International Group at the MuchMusic Video Awards,
Best Group Video at the MTV Video Music Awards for "Everybody (Backstreet's Back)",
and the World's Best-Selling Dance Artist at the World Music Awards

1999

They won the awards for the year: Music Video of the Year at the Teen Choice Awards
for "All I Have To Give", Favorite Song at the Nickelodeon Kids Choice Awards for "Everybody (Backstreet's Back)",
and the favorite Pop/Rock Band, Duo or Group at the American Music Awards.
They also won the award Billboard Music Awards, winning Artist of the Year, Album of
the Year, Album Artist of the Year, and Album Artist Duo/Group of the Year

2000

They released their 4th album, "Black and Blue", selling over 1.6 million copies
in the US in the first week, it soon later certified platinum.

2006

Kevin Richardson announces he is leaving Backstreet Boys.

2011

The boy band supergroup, NKOTBSB, is officially formed,
The NKOTBSB album is released; the album debuts at number seven in the US

2012

Kevin Richardson returns to the band.
Backstreet Boys performs on Good Morning America's Summer Concert Series as a five-piece.
This is the largest crowd GMA has ever had.

2013

The band holds a special 20th Anniversary Fan Celebration live from Hollywood.
The band's first album since Kevin rejoined, 'In A World Like This,' is released.
The album debuted at number five on the US Billboard 200, making the Backstreet Boys the
first act since Sade to have nine US top 10 albums.

2014

The Backstreet Boys won their first MTV Movie Award for Best Musical Moment for
their appearance in 'This Is The End.'
BSB are inducted into the Walk of Fame in Locarno, Switzerland.

2015

Brian & Kevin inducted into the Kentucky Music Hall of Fame.

2018

The Backstreet Boys releases "Don't Go Breaking My Heart," their first single in five years.

2022

A Very Backstreet Christmas, Backstreet Boys's tenth album and the band's
first Christmas album, is released. It entered the Billboard Top Holiday
Albums chart at number 1, and debuted at number 17 on the Billboard 200 chart.

2024

The Backstreet Boys celebrate their 30th anniversary with fans in Cancun.

2025

The Millennium 2.0 is released.
BSB appear on the Today Show to announce Millennium 2.0 will be released on July 11th,
to celebrate the 25th anniversary of their chart-topping, 5-time GRAMMY- nominated album, Millennium.
